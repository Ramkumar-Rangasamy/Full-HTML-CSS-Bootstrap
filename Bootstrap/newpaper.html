iB Cricket announces world's first Virtual Reality Cricket League

In a virtual reality cricketing world, a player can choose his or her best-loved stadium, select the pitch and can also be very specific about the type of bowling styles to face. The player will get to stand right in the middle of a world-class stadium, and hit sixes and fours just like in a real match situation. The crowd cheers and celebrates every boundary with real time commentary following the action.
